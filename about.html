About Me

As long as I can remember, I have been fascinated by computers and their capabilities.

I wrote my first line of HTML when I was in the fifth grade. However, it wasn't until high school that I realized I wanted a career in software development. I took the AP Computer Science course and immediately fell in love with programming and the logic behind it.

I completed some coursework in Computer Science at the University of North Carolina - Wilmington, then signed up for Momentum Learning, a web development boot camp in Durham, NC, to jump-start my career. Fresh out of the program, I am ready to undertake new challenges and contribute my knowledge and skills to a company.

I am currently living in Raleigh, NC. When I'm not in front a computer, I enjoy cooking, mountain biking, and playing basketball.

About Momentum Learning

Momentum Learning is an immersive, three-month web development program in Durham, NC. The course primarily utilized Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Topics covered include AGILE development, web applications, databases, version control, object-oriented programming, test-driven development, and pair programming, among many others.
